harry potter is a series of seven fantasy novels written by British author J.K Rowling. The novels chronicle the lives of a young wizard, Harry Potter,and his friends Hermoine Granger and Ron Wheasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. The main story arc concerns Harry's struggle against Lord Voldemort,a dark wizard who intends tobecome immortal,overthrow the wizard governing body known as the Ministry of Magicand subjugate all wizards and Muggles(non-magical people).
